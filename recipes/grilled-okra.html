Grilled Okra

Descriiption

These grilled okra are crispy, spicy, and really good. Don't worry if they get too charred - they delicious that way!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh okra
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • ¼ cup Cajun seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Roll okra in melted butter and then in Cajun seasoning. Cook okra on the preheated grill until charred, about 2 minutes per side.
